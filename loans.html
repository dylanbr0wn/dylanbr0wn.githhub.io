LoansBusiness and Commercial

Learn more
Whatever the reason, we have a loan for you.
Qualifund guarantees loan solutions that work for New Zealand business owners. We have a range of loan options that will suit any size and type of business. Whether wanting to bridge finance, manage cash flow, purchasing inventory, update technology or fund marketing, there are many reasons that a business may want extra funds.
We are flexible.
With less hoops to go through, and greater flexibility regarding loan options, get in contact with us today to discuss how you can save your time and energy to put towards the growth of your business.

Loan terms: up to 36 months
Loan Amount: $5000 - $1,000,000
Do I qualify? What do I need?
In order to qualify for funding, you must meet these criteria:
  • Business registered and operating in New Zealand
  • Not a start-up or new business (at least 6 months old)
  • At least $10,000 in sales recorded each month

You must provide proof that your business is registered and operates in New Zealand and that your total monthly sales are at least $10,000
