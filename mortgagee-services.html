Non-Bank Lending

Non-bank lending is when a loan is obtained from an institution other than a bank, such as a Credit Union or Building Society or private money. In some cases, the non-bank lender receives its funding from a bank!

For all New Zealanders with low deposit, self-employment, or less than ideal credit history, it can be very difficult to find a bank able to approve their home loan application. If you are in this situation, the non-bank lending provides you with a solution.

Offering mortgage solutions where others normally cannot, we look at each client individually to work out a finance solution that works for them.

If you have difficulty proving your income or short term self-employment, have adverse entries on Veda, or your credit file is shot to pieces, do not worry - Qualifund can point you in the right direction.
